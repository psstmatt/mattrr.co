Project Details

Enterprise Search

Enterprise Search, one of my earlier jobs at The Boeing Company, is comprised of a small team of intelligent people trying to change the way people search for content throughout the enterprise.

There are many things in the pipeline that we’re working on, a new feedback mechanism for the site, content adjustments, responsive layouts, retina ready assets, I can go on. What you're seeing right now is a good foundation to build upon for the new platform. Keep that in mind before giving me your extremely useful, constructive feedback. 

It’s been a super fun on-going project and in general I’m pretty happy with the visual language and how it reflects the simplicity of the product. In early 2014, we released a refined version of Search and got nothing but good feedback in regards to the user experience. I think it was another natural move in the overall evolution of this enterprise wide search platform that is used by over 90k users per week.

I want to thank  David Baughman  for his tenure at Enterprise Search and helping set the foundational road-map for the service.
